Ride Owner

Register and login to request a ride and become the owner of the ride. Requesting a ride involves specifying a destination address, an estimated arrival time, the number of total passengers in your party, and optionally, a vehicle type and any other special requests.

Driver

Register and login to search for nearby open ride requests and claim the requests to start ride service. The required vehicle information for registration includes the type, license plate number, maximum number of passengers.

Ride Sharer

Register and login to search for existing open ride requests by specifying a destination, arrival window and number of passengers in your party. You can then become a ride sharer by joining that ride.
